Breathing Room Center

Breathing Room is a yoga and holistic arts studio based in Warren County, NJ. They host a large variety of events in two different spaces and have a quickly growing list of clients and teachers.

The old website was an all in one solution purchased from VistaPrint. The plan limited the site to 5 pages, which forced Breathing Room to cram lots of content into a very small space. Site templates were limited, SEO was non-existent, and VistaPrint was attempting to up-sell for virtually every desirable feature.

The owner of the studio is very hands on and wanted a system that would allow her to grow her site as she saw fit, not as the terms of her agreement with a provider allowed. After a period of discovery with the client, we decided on a Wordpress solution.

Wordpress has a number of plug-ins that would immediately accomplish her most needed features. Contact forms, a calendar, and the ability to finely control the look and layout of the content on the site were big plusses for Wordpress.

The main geeky details were the use of a custom theme based on the 960.gs CSS framework, and the use of Wordpress' underused "Custom Fields" feature to protect the Google Map on the directions page from being altered by Wordpress' TinyMCE editor whenever the client wanted to edit the Directions page.

I ended up extracting a Wordpress theme framework from this build that lives here on Github. Many thanks to Nathan Smith for the 960.gs and Elliot Jay Stocks for upgrading the excellent Starkers theme to include the many awesome features that WP 3.0 offers.

Project Details:

  • Wordpress, custom theme
  • Very compact CSS - loads FAST
  • Classy and judicious use of jQuery
  • Site header and content header tags make use of TypeKit fonts.
